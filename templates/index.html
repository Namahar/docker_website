Me

My name is Saeed Rahaman and I am an aspiring Computer Engineer. I am a recent graduate from the University of Central Florida in Orlando, Florida for my masters in Computer Engineering with an interest in artificial intelligence within the automotive and medical industries.

I am passionate about programming and developing simple and elegant solutions to solve large scale problems. I believe that artificial intelligence can revolutionize the world in a variety of areas. AI can be used to create self-driving cars to improve traffic congestion and reduce automotive deaths. AI can also be utilized to provide better healthcare to ill patients. My goal is to help develop these types of systems that can improve the daily lives of people.

My other hobbies include building PCs and restoring and modifying cars. I've taken apart and completely rebuilt a 1988 Pontiac Fiero. I also enjoy modifying my 2020 Honda Civic Si. Taking apart machines and analyzing how each part works allows me to understand concepts easier and it's interesting to see how different systems are designed.
